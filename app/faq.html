Frequently Asked Questions

Is this gambling?

[PLACEHOLDER] No. The Hawk Games operates skill-based prize competitions. All entries require you to answer a skill-based question, which makes it a non-gambling competition compliant with the UK Gambling Act 2005.

How do I enter?

[PLACEHOLDER] Simply browse to a competition you like, correctly answer the skill question, select the number of tickets you want, and complete the checkout. That's it!

Can I enter for free?

[PLACEHOLDER] Yes. We are required by law to offer a free entry route. Please see our "Free Entry Route" page for full details on how to submit a postal entry.

How is the winner chosen?

[PLACEHOLDER] Once a competition ends, all valid entries (both paid and free) are included in a final draw. A winner is then chosen at random using a provably fair method.
