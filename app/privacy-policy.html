What data we collect

  • Identity and contact data (name, date of birth, email, phone, address)

  • Prize fulfilment data (delivery details, scheduling)

Purposes & lawful bases

We process personal data for:

  • Running competitions and administering entries (performance of contract)
  • Taking payment and preventing fraud (performance of contract; legitimate interests; legal obligations)
  • Age/identity verification and sanctions screening for winners (legitimate interests; legal obligations)
  • Customer support and service messages (performance of contract)
  • Analytics and service improvement (legitimate interests)
  • Marketing about our own products/services (consent for electronic mail under PECR or soft opt‑in where applicable; you can opt out at any time)
  • Compliance with law and regulator requests (legal obligations)

Direct marketing (email/SMS)

We only send electronic direct marketing to individuals where we have your consent or where the “soft opt‑in” applies for our own similar products/services. You can withdraw consent or opt out at any time via unsubscribe links or by contacting us. We do not sell your data to third parties for their marketing.

Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies/SDKs for essential functions, analytics and personalised content/ads. See our Cookie Policy for details and choices.

Sharing your data

We share data with trusted processors who help deliver our services (e.g., payment providers, hosting, analytics, communications, ID verification and fulfilment). Where required by law, we may share data with regulators or law enforcement. We do not permit processors to use your data for their own marketing.

International transfers

If we transfer data outside the UK/EEA, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g., adequacy regulations or standard contractual clauses).

Retention

We keep account, entry and transaction records for up to 7 years for tax and audit. Other data is retained only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law.

Your rights

You have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to processing, and to data portability. You may also object to direct marketing at any time. To exercise rights, contact privacy@the-hawk-games.co.uk. You can complain to the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Security

We apply administrative, technical and organisational measures to protect data (including encryption in transit, access controls, monitoring and incident response). No internet transmission is 100% secure; any transmission is at your own risk.

Children

Our services are for 18+ only. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
